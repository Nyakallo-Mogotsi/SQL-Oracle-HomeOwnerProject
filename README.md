  SQL-Oracle-HomeOwnerProject
Service management system featuring online booking, contractor management, real-time inventory tracking, and automated business reporting for handyman services

🎯 Objectives
- Provide a user-friendly booking system for handyman services.
- Automate contractor assignment based on location, availability, and skills.
- Maintain accurate inventory tracking for tools and materials.
- Enable secure electronic payment processing.
- Collect and manage customer feedback efficiently.
- Generate business reports to support decision-making and growth.
- 
⚙️ System Scope
The database system supports:
-   Service Requests  : Online and phone-based booking with emergency prioritization.
-   Contractor Management  : Job assignment, workload distribution, and performance tracking.
-   Inventory Management  : Stock monitoring, usage tracking, and replenishment.
-   Payment Processing  : Secure billing and transaction handling.
-   Customer Feedback  : Ratings, reviews, and performance insights.

🚧 Constraints
- Services limited to home repair, renovation, and maintenance (no large-scale construction).
- Operations restricted to defined geographical areas.
- Internet access required for online reservations and payments.
- Contractor availability may affect response times.
- Compliance with local labor laws, safety regulations, and data privacy standards.

📊 Database System Goals
- Improve customer retention through a fast, intuitive interface.
- Handle day-to-day transactions reliably.
- Track company assets and prevent misuse.
- Support emergency service requests with priority handling.
- Provide secure storage of client information.
- Generate relevant reports for business growth.
